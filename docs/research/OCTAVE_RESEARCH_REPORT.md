## Executive Summary

OctaveHQ is a **Generative Go-To-Market (GTM) platform** that uses AI agents and a dynamic context engine to automate and personalize B2B sales and marketing workflows. Their core innovation is transforming static ICP (Ideal Customer Profile) definitions into real-time, context-aware engines that power hyper-personalized outbound campaigns at scale.

**Key Insight:** Octave moves beyond template-based outbound (what they call "Mad Libs") to a generative approach where AI agents research, qualify, and write sequences with deep contextual understanding.

## üéØ CORE PRODUCT: The GTM Context Engine

### What It Is
The **GTM Context Engine** is the heart of OctaveHQ. It's a dynamic, real-time system that:
- Ingests public data and internal content (docs, decks, libraries, tribal knowledge)
- Transforms static ICP definitions into living, breathing context
- Continuously updates with market shifts, product changes, and competitive intelligence
- Powers AI agents that can research, qualify, and create messaging with unparalleled relevancy

### The Problem They Solve
**Pain Point #1: ICP Docs Are Digital Tombstones**
- Positioning documents become outdated quickly
- Messaging drifts off-brand
- No single source of truth for what makes outbound relevant

**Pain Point #2: Templates Turn Precision into Mad Libs**
- Static templates with {{tags}} can't adapt to shifting strategies
- Rich market signals become generic snippets
- Personalization is superficial

**Pain Point #3: Workflows Become Prompt Bottlenecks**
- Each campaign requires dozens of fragile prompts
- Brittle chains break as markets shift
- Growth ideas get stifled by technical limitations

### The Solution: "Generative GTM"
Instead of:
- Static templates
- A/B subject line tests
- Pseudo {{personalization}}

Octave provides:
- Real-time context that adapts to market conditions
- AI agents that understand your product deeply
- One-to-one personalized sequences without heavy prompting
- Intelligent qualification using natural language

## üéì Key Concepts to Understand

### 1. Workspaces
- Container for organizing GTM strategies
- Each workspace represents a different GTM motion or product line
- Limits vary by tier (2-6 active, unlimited created)

### 2. Offerings
- Your product/service definitions within a workspace
- What you're selling and to whom
- Limits vary by tier (1-3 active per workspace)

### 3. Elements
- Building blocks of your GTM strategy
- Could be personas, pain points, value props, use cases
- Limits vary by tier (12-48 active per workspace)

### 4. Playbooks
- Complete GTM strategies that combine offerings and elements
- Generate messaging, sequences, and campaigns
- Limits vary by tier (2-16 active per workspace)

### 5. Generation Credits
- Currency for AI-powered operations
- Used for research, qualification, message generation
- Range: 100 credits (Base) to 60,000+ (Turbo/Ultra)

- Your company's tone, style, and messaging guidelines
- Ensures consistent output across all generated content
- Can be refined and trained

## üìã Appendix: Terminology Glossary

- **GTM (Go-To-Market):** Strategy for bringing products to market and reaching customers
- **ICP (Ideal Customer Profile):** Definition of the perfect customer for your product
- **Outbound:** Proactive sales approach (cold emails, LinkedIn, calls)
- **Sequences:** Series of automated touchpoints with prospects
- **SDR (Sales Development Rep):** Team member focused on outbound prospecting
- **Sequencer:** Tool for automating multi-touch campaigns (Outreach, Salesloft)
- **Orchestration:** Coordinating multiple tools and workflows
- **Context Engine:** System that maintains and applies relevant business context
- **Generative GTM:** Using AI to create (not template) personalized marketing/sales content
- **Mad Libs:** Template-based personalization with simple variable substitution
- **Tribal Knowledge:** Undocumented expertise that exists in team members' heads
